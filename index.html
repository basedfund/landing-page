Bedrock protects your DeFi assets

Protect your DeFi tokens against key risks like smart contract hacks by depositing assets into a Bedrock Safe.

Why

In 2021, criminal hackers stole approximately $3.2 billion in cryptocurrency —and the victims are disproportionately in DeFi.

Problem

Existing DeFi insurance solutions mimic traditional institutions. Claim incentives are misaligned, and premium pricing is ad-hoc.

How we are different

On BedRock you deposit Defi assets to pool and spread risk with like-minded users and protocols.

Users are both the insured and insurer.

How it works

Users deposit yield bearing assets into a Safe. If a hack occurs on the users Defi protocol. Other Safes are partially liquidated and funds are shared with users who filed claims .
